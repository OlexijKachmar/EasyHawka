ABOUT PROJECT

Having some problems with deciding where to go at a break time? That’s where Easy HAWKA come in handy! We gathered eating places all around Lviv Polytechnic National University, so that tired students can rest and regain the ability to continue their studying. Do not test the fate, use Easy HAWKA. Only for NULP students, but we can bargain…

Instruction: Firstly, choose your location due to academic building. Then choose your financial ability from low to high. You will receive the list of suitable places. You can also use additional filters to narrow your results amount according to your wishes. Final list contains places sorted by your filters, only thins left to do is choose one place and have a nice time there
